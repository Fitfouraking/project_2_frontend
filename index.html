Drink up and spread the love!


Like most brilliant ideas, the lifeblood of Tappster came to fruition over a fresh pint of Guiness. Our mission is to make your life easier by providing you the perfect beer drinker's tool - be it a preemptive peek at your dinner reservation's beer list to find the perfect pairing or scouting that elusive pint of your favorite craft brew in a foreign city - Tapptser is the one stop resource for the avid beer connoisseur to the everyday barley pop adventurer. Search. Contribute. Drink. Repeat. Cheers...
